ABOUT ME

Biography

Hello, I'm Sanusi Islamiyat Oreoluwa, a passionate and driven individual with a rich background in Agriculture from the University of Ilorin, Ilorin, Kwara state, Nigeria. My academic roots may be in agriculture, but my true calling lies in the tech world. I embarked on this journey in October 2022, drawn to crafting user-centered designs that drive business success.

As I delved deeper into the world of tech, I discovered a new passion for Front-End Engineering in 2023. I realized that bringing designs to life and
creating functional, interactive products was an essential part of the tech ecosystem. This revelation sparked a desire to learn and grow in this field.
To fuel my growth, I sought out a supportive community, authentic learning platforms, and like-minded individuals who share my enthusiasm for tech.
That's when I found AltSchool Africa, where I'm currently a student with the ID ALT/SOE/024/0462 . I'm excited to be part of a community that fosters growth, accountability, and motivation.
Through my journey, I aim to leverage my skills in UI/UX design and Front-End Engineering to create innovative solutions that make a meaningful impact.
